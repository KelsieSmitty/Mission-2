Peanut's Bio ✍

Every good girls story deserves to be told!



The life of Peanut:


Peanut is a 9 year old Chocolate and White Cocker Spaniel. This beautiful girl was bred in Christchurch and moved up to Clevedon, Auckland as a puppy at 8 weeks old. Her new forever home parents, Kelsie and Robbie, took her in, where she also met her new sister, Chocolah, a burmese cat.

Peanut spent her early days playing with the other dogs and puppies at Clevedon, attending puppy school and falling asleep on the bed after day 2. At puppy school she was much less interested in playing with other dogs, but escaping out of the little fences to go snuggle some humans and see if they had any treats on offer. Her obedience was top tier, especially if cocktail sausages were involved, and she soon pogressed on to doing some agility when she got bigger and stronger.

The rest of her adult days have involved taking over Kelsie and Robbie's bed, begging for food from her new little toddler humans, going for walkies and protecting the house from the passerbys that dare to walk past.
